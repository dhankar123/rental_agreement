Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I add a new property?

Navigate to the **Add New Property** link in the sidebar menu and fill out the required details including name, location, and rent.

How can I record a rent payment?

Go to the **Payments** page and click the "+ Record Payment" button at the top right. Select the tenant, property, amount, and date.

What should I do if a tenant's status is incorrect?

Go to **Manage Tenants** or click the "View" link on the specific property card. You can edit the tenant details and update their status there.
